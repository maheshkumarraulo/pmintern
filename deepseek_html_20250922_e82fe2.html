Empowering Youth Through Internships

Join the PM Internship Scheme and gain real-world experience

Eligibility Criteria

Age 21-24

Applicants must be between 21 and 24 years of age at the time of application.

Indian Citizen

Must be a citizen of India with valid proof of nationality.

Education

Must have completed at least secondary education from a recognized board.

Employment Status

Not currently in full-time employment or education during the internship period.

Internship Benefits

Monthly Stipend

₹5,000 per month (₹4,500 from government + ₹500 from company)

One-time Grant

₹6,000 one-time grant for learning materials and expenses

Insurance Coverage

Comprehensive health and accident insurance for the internship period

Internship Certificate

Official certificate upon successful completion of the internship

Industry Exposure

Gain valuable industry experience and professional networking opportunities

Mentorship

Guidance from industry experts and professionals throughout the internship

Internship Categories

Information Technology

Software development, data analysis, cybersecurity, and more

Finance

Banking, accounting, financial analysis, and investment

Healthcare

Medical research, healthcare administration, pharmaceuticals

Manufacturing

Engineering, production management, quality control
